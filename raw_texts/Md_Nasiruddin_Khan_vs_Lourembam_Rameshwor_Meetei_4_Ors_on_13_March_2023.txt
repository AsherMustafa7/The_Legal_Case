After hearing the submission advanced by the learned senior counsel appearing for the parties and
after careful perusal of the record, the following issues are framed:-
(1) Whether the non-disclosure of the petitioner's non-
agricultural land under Dag No. 728-640 corresponding to Patta No. 1024, 403 Village Sahar
Ulubari, Part-II Manza Ulubari Assam, in the relevant column under Part B Para 7 (ii) of the Form
26 affidavit filed by the respondent No. 1 along with his nomination paper in connection with the
12th Manipur Legislative Assembly Election, 2022 would materially affect the election of the
respondent No. 1 ?
