B E F O R E HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 28-11-2022 Heard Mr. Ajoy
Pebam, learned counsel appearing for the applicant; Mr. ST Kom, learned counsel for respondent
No. 1; Mr. N. Mahendra, learned counsel for respondent No. 2 and Mr. Devan Sapam, learned
counsel for respondent No. 3.
The present application has been filed seeking leave for allowing the applicant to file replication to
the written statement filed by respondent No. 1 in the connected Election Recr. Petition No. 8 of
2022. Mr. ST Kom, learned contesting counsel (respondent No. 1) as well as Mr. N. Mahendra,
learned counsel and Mr. Devan Sapam, learned counsel for respondent Nos. 2 & 3 respectively
submitted that they have no objection in allowing the present application.
On hearing the submissions advanced by the learned counsel appearing for the parties and on
perusal of the records, this Court is of the considered view that sufficient ground has been made out
to allow the present application.
Accordingly, the present application is allowed. Registry is directed to put on record the replication
in the connected file.
Misc. case stands closed.
H JUDGE Dhakeshori
