Heard with the aid of case diary.
This is First application of the applicant Smt. Saroj Singh filed under section 439 Cr.P.C. for grant of
bail. The applicant was arrested on 08.07.2019 in connection with Crime No.271/2019 registered at
Police Station Ramnagar, District Satna for the offence punishable under Sections 498A, 304B, 34
of IPC and Section 3/4 of Dowry Prohibition Act.
As per prosecution case on 24.06.2019 deceased Nidhi Singh wife of co-accused Uday Bhan Singh
committed suicide by hanging herself. It is alleged that applicant Smt. Saroj Singh, mother-in-law
and co-accused Uday Bhan Singh, husband of the deceased demanded one fridge and one box and
harass her and co-accused Uday Bhan Singh assaulted her due to which she committed suicide.
Learned counsel for the applicant submits that applicant is innocent and has falsely been implicated
in the offence. The applicant is mother- in-law of the deceased Nidhi Singh. The allegation regarding
demand of dowry are general in nature. Charge sheet has been filed. The applicant is in custody
since 08.07.2019 and the conclusion of trial is likely to take long time, hence prayed for release of
the applicant on bail.
Learned counsel for the State opposed the prayer and submitted that applicant is mother-in-law of
the deceased who harassed the deceased Nidhi Singh, due to which, she committed suicide within
six months of her marriage. So, she should not be released on bail.
Looking to fact and circumstances of the case and the fact that the applicant is mother-in-law of the
deceased Nidhi Singh and the allegation of demand of dowry are general in nature, charge sheet has
been filed, applicant is in custody since 08.07.2019 and conclusion of trial will take time, without
commenting on the merits of the case, the application is allowed and it is directed that the applicant
Smt. Saroj Singh be released on bail upon her furnishing personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/-
