ORDER
13.09.2023 The Court of Judicial Magistrate 1st Class, R. S. Pura vide its order dated 08.02.2021 on
file no. 425/Misc, came to grant an amount of Rs. 4,000/- per month as maintenance in favour of
the respondent. The maintenance was payable by the petitioner no. 1 - Sher Singh, who is her
husband. The petitioner no. 1 joined by his father as the petitioner no. 2, his mother as the petitioner
no. 3 and his sister as the respondent no. 4 in this petition are impugning the course of proceedings
initiated by the respondent under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2015.
This Court in terms of an interim order dated 20.04.2022 came to direct stay of proceedings before
the Judicial Magistrate 1st Class, R. S. Pura.
Learned counsel for the respondent submits that the respondent is in a state of destitution not
having even the money to defend herself in the present case.
