ORDER
10.05.2023 The petitioner/applicant seeks transfer of civil suit filed by the
respondent/non-applicant herein for restitution of conjugal rights from the court of Additional
District Judge, Rajouri to another court of competent jurisdiction at Jammu.
Learned counsel for the petitioner/applicant would submit that there are two more cases pending
before the court of Special Railway Magistrate, Jammu in File No. DV/683/2021 titled as
"Rukhsana Kouser and another vs Mohd Rafiq and others" filed under section 23 of J&K Protection
of Women Domestic Violence Act, 2010 and the other pending before Principal Judge, Family Court,
Jammu in File No. 562/Misc. titled as "Rukhsana Kouser and another vs Mohd Rafiq" filed under
section 125 Cr.P.C.
Learned counsel for the petitioner/applicant would submit that the marriage between the petitioner
and respondent herein is intact and out of the wedlock, a daughter has been born and is living with
the petitioner/applicant.
In view of the aforesaid position, before proceeding to deal with the instant petition on merits, it is
deemed appropriate to summon the respondent in order to explore a possibility of reconciliation
between the parties.
