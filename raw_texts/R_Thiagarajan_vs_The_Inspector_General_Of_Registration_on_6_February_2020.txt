The learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner seeks permission of this Court to
withdraw this petition as the core issue in this case has been answered by the Hon'ble Full Bench of

the petition.
2.Permission is granted. Recording the endorsement made by the learned counsel for the petitioner,
this Writ Petition is dismissed as withdrawn. No costs. Consequently, connected miscellaneous
petition is closed.
