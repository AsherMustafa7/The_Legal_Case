BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 24.11.2022 Heard Mr. P.
Praveenkumar, learned counsel appearing for the applicant, Mr. Ajoy Pebam, learned counsel
appearing for respondent No. 1 and Mr. N. Kumarjit, learned senior counsel assisted by Mr. N.
Zequeson, learned counsel appearing for respondent No. 2.
The present application had been filed seeking leave for allowing the applicant to file replication to
the written statement filed by respondent No. 1 in El.Recr.Pet. No. 4 of 2022.
Mr. Ajoy Pebam, learned counsel appearing for the contesting respondent No. 1 as well as Mr. N.
Kumarjit, learned senior counsel appearing for respondent No. 2, submitted that they have no
objection in allowing the present application.
In view of the submission made above and on perusal of the present application, this Court finds no
ground for not allowing the present application. Accordingly, the present application is allowed.
Registry is directed to put on record the said recrimination petition in the connected record.
Misc. case stands closed.
