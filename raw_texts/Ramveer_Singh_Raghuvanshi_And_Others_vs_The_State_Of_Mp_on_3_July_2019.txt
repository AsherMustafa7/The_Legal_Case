With the consent, heard finally.
The present petition has been preferred by the petitioner seeking direction for consideration of the
case of the petitioner whereby respondents in not granting revised pay scale of the petitioners after
completion of 24 years of services in the light of the order by coordinate Bench of this Court in the
case of Ram Swaroop Bhoj and others Vs. State of M.P. and others passed in W.P. No. 8733/2019
and same applies squarely in fact situation of case.
Learned counsel for the petitioner confines his argument to the extend that a direction may be
issued for consideration of his representation as per law which is to be filed by the petitioner.
Petitioners pray for similar relief as has been given by the coordinate Bench of this Court in the case
of Ram Swaroop Bhoj and others Vs. State of M.P. and others in W.P. No.8733/2019 vide order
dated 29.04.2019 has passed the following direction as under:-
"(i) The petitioners are directed to prefer fresh representations containing all the
necessary material facts to demonstrate parity of their cases with the case of Smt.
Prerna (supra) within a period of one month from today;
(ii) The concerning respondents-authorities on receipt of the said representations,
are directed to decide the same in accordance with law within a period of 60 days
thereafter from the date of receipt of said representations along with a certified copy
of this order;
(iii) In case, the petitioners are found to be similarly placed when compared with the
case of Smt. Prerna (supra), the benefit as 2 WP-12461-2019 admissible to them in
terms of Smt. Prerna's case be extended to them within a further period of 60 days;
and
(iv) In case the competent authority does not find parity as aforesaid, then,
representations of the petitioner be decided by speaking order and the same be

On the other hand, learned counsel for respondent has no objection to the innocuous prayer made
by the petitioner.
In the facts and circumstances of the case and maintaining parity with the order passed in W.P. No.
8733/2019 (Supra), the petition is disposed of in terms of order dated 29.04.2019 passed in W.P.
No. 8733/2019 (Ram Swaroop Bhoj and others Vs. State of M.P.).
Petition stands disposed of in above terms.
