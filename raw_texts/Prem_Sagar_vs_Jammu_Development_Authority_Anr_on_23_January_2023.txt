ORDER
01. Petitioner has preferred this writ petition under Article 226 of the Constitution of India seeking a
direction to respondents to accord permission for transfer of lease hold rights of Plot No. 102,
Sector-6 measuring 400'x 80' situated at Trikuta Nagar, Housing Colony, Jammu.
02. The petitioner claims to have purchased the lease hold rights of Plot No. 102, Sector-6
measuring 400' x 80' situated at Trikuta Nagar, Housing Colony, Jammu from one Smt. Naresh
Choudhary and become lessee of the said plot and that the requisite agreement with regard to the
same has also been executed with respondents on 26.11.2007. The petitioner being fully eligible and
had duly applied for transfer of lease hold rights and completed all the requisite formalities and
submitted the same to the respondents.
03. It was alleged that the respondents are sitting over the matter and delaying the same without
any rhyme and reason leaving no other option for the petitioners except to approach this Court
invoking writ jurisdiction. It has been finally prayed that the respondents be commanded to
discharge their legal obligation by granting due permission for transfer of the lease hold rights of the
respective plot in favour of the petitioner as per the transfer policy of Jammu Development
Authority.
04. Heard. Issue notice to the respondents.
05. Notice waived by Mr. Adarsh Sharma, learned GA on behalf of respondents.

06. Learned counsel for the petitioner at this stage, submits that the petition can be considered to be
disposed of at this stage with a direction to the respondents Authority to consider the matter on its
own merits with regard to the plea raised by the petitioner in this petition. Learned counsel for the
respondents did not object and agreed that the petitioner's plea be considered.
07. In view of pleadings of the parties and submissions made by the learned counsels for the parties,
this petition is disposed of with a direction to the respondents to consider the plea raised by the
petitioner in this petition within three months from the date a copy of this order is made available to
them, by passing a speaking order. The petitioner, however, shall make up all the requirements for
transfer of lease hold rights in favour of prospective transferees, as required under law.
08. The petition, is accordingly, disposed of along with pending application(s), if any.
