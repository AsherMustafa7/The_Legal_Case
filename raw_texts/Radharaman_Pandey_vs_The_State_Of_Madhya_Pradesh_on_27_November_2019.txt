Shri K. N. Bundela, learned Govt. Advocate for the respondents/ State.
Heard on admission and interim relief.
Counsel for the petitioner submits that petitioner was appointed as Salesman in the respondent
No.4- Society in the year 2014. It is submitted that in pursuant to the general order dated 18.10.2019
issued by the Commissioner, Cooperative Societies, Bhopal and also the letter dated 18.11.2019
issued by the General Manager, District Cooperative Central Bank Ltd., Rewa, his services have been
terminated by the Society without giving any show cause notice and opportunity of hearing.
Counsel for the petitioner submits that the competent authority is Board of Director of the Society
but in the present case the power has been usurped by the Higher Authority. He placed reliance on
the order passed by the Coordinate Bench on 25.1.2018 in W.P. No.1497/2018 ( Vinay Upadhyay Vs.
The State of Madhya Pradesh).
Considering the aforesaid submissions, issue notice to the respondents on payment of P.F. within 7
working days by both modes, failing which this petition shall stand dismissed without reference to
the Court. Notice be made returnable within four weeks.
Till the next date of hearing the operation of the impugned order dated 18.11.2019 terminating the
services of the petitioner, shall remain stayed.
List this matter for analogous hearing along with W.P. No.1497/2018. C.C. as per rules.
(VIJAY KUMAR SHUKLA) JUDGE mrs. mishra 2 WP-25050-2019
