ORDER
31.05.2023 Case set up by the petitioner is that Police Station Udhampur registered FIR No.
215/2023 for offences under Section 418/420 IPC and accused no. 2-Gaurav Arora in the said FIR is
an employee of the petitioner company. It is further case of the petitioner company that it facilitated
a deal between respondents No. 3 and 4 for sale and distribution of Vedic Mathematic Software as
well as providing training classes on the approach of respondent no. 3 and pursuant to the said deal,
an agreement dated 27.08.2022 came to be executed between respondents 3 and 4. After lodgement
of FIR, respondent no. 2- SHO Police Station, Udhampur freezed the bank account of the petitioner
company. The petitioner company moved an application before learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,
Udhampur for defreezing the bank account and learned CJM vide impugned order dated
04.05.2023 defreezed the bank account of the petitioner subject, however, to the condition that
petitioner shall furnish the bank security/guarantee in the amount of Rs. 15,55,400/-.
Short grievance of the petitioner is that the controversy involved in the FIR, in question, is
essentially a civil dispute in nature, however, respondent no. 4-the complainant has filed the
aforesaid FIR as a tool for the recovery of the amount.
A case for interim indulgence is made out.
Notice to the respondents, returnable within four weeks. Requisite steps for service be taken within
one week.
List on 10.07.2023.
