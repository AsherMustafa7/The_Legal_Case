BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A. GUNESHWAR SHARMA Order 21.02.2023 Present Mr. Juno
Rahman, learned counsel on behalf of the petitioner and Mr. RK. Umakanta, learned PP on behalf of
the respondents.
The present petition under Section 482 filed by the petitioner herein praying the Superintendent of
Police, Thoubal and Officer-In- Charge, Yairipok Police Station for proper investigation of FIR Case
No. 26(06)2020 YPK P.S. U/S 498-A/306 IPC and also to change the case punishable under Section
302/34 IPC. The petitioner is the father of the deceased and newly impleaded respondent No. 4 is
the husband of the deceased. In the FIR, it is mentioned that it was a case of suicide and not a case
of murder.
On 02.02.2023, Mr. RK. Umakanta, learned PP for the State respondents, sought some time to
ascertain whether a final report has been filed in the present case or not.
Today, the learned PP submitted a letter dated 12.02.2023 sent to him by Sr. Superintendent of
Police, Thoubal, stating that the Charge Sheet No. 02/2023/YPK-PS dated 09.02.2023 has been
handed over to this Court and they have prepared a Charge Sheet No. 02/2023/YPK -PS U/S
498-A/306 IPC corresponding with UD case No. 01/YPK - PS/2020 u/s 174 Cr.PC. Since, the final
report under section 173 has been prepared and is the process of submission to the concerned Court,
the main prayer of the petitioner in this petition is substantially complied. The petitioner is
permitted to raise any other point before the Trial Court as per law.
With this observation, the present petition is disposed of. No cost.
