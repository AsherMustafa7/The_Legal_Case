ORDER
29.11.2023 Heard Mr. Anand Grover, learned senior counsel assisted by Mr. S. Worthing, learned
counsel appearing for the petitioner.
Issue notice in both the petitions as well as the connected stay application, returnable within 1 (one)
week.
It has been submitted by the learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner that the petitioner
is ready and willing to corporate with the investigation conducted by the I.O. of the FIR in question
and that that petitioner has also got interim order from this Court to the fact that no coercive action
should be taken against him in connection with other FIR and complaint made against the
petitioner. The learned senior counsel accordingly made a prayer for passing similar order.
Mr. Ms. Jyotsana, learned counsel assisting Mr. M. Devananda, learned Addl. AG entered
appearance and accepts notice on behalf of all the respondents, hence, no formal notice is called for.
Mr. M. Devananda, learned Add. AG submitted that he will file counter affidavit within 1 (one) week
and that prayer for passing for interim order be considered after 1 (one) week.

It is made clear that if the petitioner violates the conditions mentioned herein above, the
respondents are at liberty to approach this Court for modification and cancellation of this interim
order.
List these cases again on 13.12.2023.

respondents in the cause list.
