ORDER
15.03.2023 Mr. Pranav Kohli, learned senior counsel submits that let the Jammu Municipal
Corporation and its technical staff take re-measurement of the building including the lift area,
mumty area and fire escape.
Therefore, the Jammu Municipal Corporation and its technical staff are directed to take
re-measurement of the building including the lift area, mumty area and fire escape as per the site
plan. Of course, for that purpose the Jammu Municipal Corporation shall de-seal the building and
after taking the re- measurement shall immediately reseal the same. The measurement shall be
taken in presence of either the petitioner or his representative.
The Jammu Municipal Corporation shall use its machinery for removal of debris, however, the
petitioner shall bear expenses for the same.
Copy of the report be furnished by or before the next date of hearing. List on 17.04.2023.
