BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A. GUNESHWAR SHARMA Order 25.10.2023 [1] Heard Ms. Th.
Ranjita, learned counsel for the petitioner. [2] The 5 (five) petitioners and 2 (two) others are accused
in FIR No. 161(8) 2023 TBL PS U/S 448/427/380/325/384/34 IPC. There is connected FIR i.e. FIR
No. 84(04) 2023 TBL-PS U/S 326 & 302 IPC against the family of the complainant.
[2] Vide order dated 13.10.2023 in Cril.Misc.(AB) Case No. 65 of 2023, the Ld. Sessions Judge,
Thoubal rejected the anticipatory bail application preferred by the present 5 (five) petitioners and 2
(two) other petitioners on the ground that the petitioners and others were not co-operating with the
investigation and they were concealing the facts of the case. Vide order dated 20.10.2023 in AB No.
39 of 2023, this Court issued notice and granted anticipatory bail to 2 (two) more accused and the
remaining 5 (five) accused who are petitioners herein approached this Court by way of the present
petition being AB No. 40 of 2023 praying for the similar relief.
[4] Issue notice.
[5] Mr. Y. Ashang, learned PP accepts notice on behalf of
respondent Nos. 1 & 3. Hence, no formal step is required to be taken with respect to these
respondents.
[6] Petitioners are directed to take steps with respect to respondent Nos. 2, 4 & 5 by speed post,
returnable on 27.11.2023. [7] Ms. Th. Ranjita, learned counsel for the petitioners, submits that

order is valid till the next date. [10] List this case before the roster bench on 27.11.2023 along with
