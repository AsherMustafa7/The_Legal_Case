1. Today Advocates are abstaining from work. I have perused the record.
2. Pursuant to the order passed by this Court dated 19.03.2025, the deponent of the petition namely
Shri Sarbojit Banerjee, Deputy Manager, The Oriental Insurance Company Ltd., Legal Hub, 16-A,
M.G. Road Civil Lines Prayagraj is present before this Court along-with the necessary instructions.
3. Shri Sarbojit Banerjee, Deputy Manager, The Oriental Insurance Company Ltd., Legal Hub, 16-A,
M.G. Road Civil Lines Prayagraj has placed his I.D. proof to the Bench Secretary of this Court and
on the basis of his I.D. Proof he placed before this Court the Demand Drafts along-with calculation
chart in the name of Sunita Devi (wife of deceased)/ Respondent No. 1/1 of Rs. 82,134/- (DD No.
020078 dated 21.03.2025 of Axis Bank), Rs. 81,103/- (DD No. 020080 dated 21.03.2025 of Axis
Bank) & Rs. 82,134/- (DD No. 020079 dated 21.03.2025 of Axis Bank).
4. The aforesaid amount was in respect of the award given by the Permanent Lok Adalat dated
02.12.2024 in Case No. 62 of 2017, Case No. 65 of 2017 and Case No. 66 of 2017 (Devendra vs. OIC).
By the aforesaid order, a direction was given to the petitioner to make the payment of Rs.70,000/- +
Rs.10,000/- litigation expenses along-with interest.
5. Pursuant to the aforesaid in respect of Case No. 62 of 2017, Case No. 65 of 2017 and Case No. 66
of 2017 (Devendra vs. OIC) the Demand Drafts along-with calculation chart were prepared in favour
of Sunita Devi/ Respondent No. 1/1, and the same is placed before this Court. The same are taken on
record.
6. After going through the same, the Court is of opinion that since the award passed by the
Permanent Lok Adalat has already been complied with by the petitioner, therefore, no further
orders are required to be passed and the present writ petition is dismissed as having become
infructuous.
7. Accordingly, the writ petition is dismissed as having become infructuous.
8. Shri Sarbojit Banerjee, Deputy Manager, The Oriental Insurance Company Ltd., Legal Hub, 16-A,
M.G. Road Civil Lines Prayagraj is taken lots of efforts to comply the order passed by this Court and
only on the basis of his efforts all the matters were finally disposed of within a period of two weeks
