Date : 03-07-2019 Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned counsel for the opposite
parties. Patna High Court MJC No.38 of 2016 dt.03-07-2019
2. The petitioners have moved the Court alleging non compliance of various orders by which the
exercise relating to affiliation of courses in their colleges was to be done by the opposite parties.
3. It appears that whatever was required to be done, as per the Statute is being done.
4. In view thereof, the applications stand disposed off as the Court finds that there is no deliberate
or willful violation or disobedience of the orders passed by the Court in the present batch of cases
and as such no contempt is made out.
