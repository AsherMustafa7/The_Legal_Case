1. Heard Sri Chandra Bhushan Prasad, learned counsel for the applicant and Sri Rajeev Kumar
Singh, learned A.G.A. for the State.
2. The present application has been filed for directing the learned Civil Judge (Junior Division),
FTC-Ist, Muzaffar Nagar to conclude and decide the trial of Complaint Case No.120 of 2016 (Smt.
Pavitra Vs. Anil & Others), arising out of proceeding u/s 12 of the Protection of Women from
Domestic Violence Act, 2005 (hereinafter referred to as 'the Domestic Violence Act'), Police
Station-Kotwali Nagar, District-Muzaffar Nagar, within stipulated period.
3. Contention of learned counsel for the applicant is that complaint case of the applicant u/s 12 of
the Domestic Violence Act has been pending since 2016, but till date it has not been decided. As per
Section 12(5) of the Domestic Violence Act, it is provided that the Magistrate shall endeavour to
dispose of every application made under this Section within a period of 60 days from the date of its
first hearing. Sections-12(4) and 12(5) of the Domestic Violence Act are being quoted below:-

"12(4). The Magistrate shall fix the first date of hearing, which shall not ordinarily be
beyond three days from the date of receipt of the application by the court.
12(5). The Magistrate shall endeavour to dispose of every application made under
sub-section (1) within a period of sixty days from the date of its first hearing."
4. In view of the above, there is a statutory mandate to decide the complaint u/s 12 of the Domestic
Violence Act within a period of 60 days from the date of its first hearing.
5. Accordingly, the learned Civil Judge (Junior Division), FTC-Ist, Muzaffar Nagar to conclude and
decide the trial of Complaint Case No.120 of 2016 (Smt. Pavitra Vs. Anil & Others), arising out of
proceeding u/s 12 of the Domestic Violence Act, Police Station-Kotwali Nagar, District-Muzaffar
Nagar, expeditiously preferably within a period of 90 days from the date of receipt of copy of this
order.
6. With the aforesaid direction, the application is disposed of.
7. This matter has been disposed of, therefore, the same may be untagged from Application u/s 482
No.18994 of 2024.
