14.07.2021 Order No.
07. 1. This matter is taken up by video conferencing mode.
2. Heard Mr. Shakti Dhar Das, learned Sr. counsel for the Petitioner and Mr. K. Gaya, learned
Additional Standing Counsel.
3. This is an application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. for grant of bail to the Petitioner Pradhani Jani in
connection with K. Nuagaon P.S. Case No.53 of 2020 corresponding to C.T. Case No.48 of 2020
pending in the court of learned Special Judge-cum-Additional Sessions Judge, Balliguda for alleged
commission of offence under Section 20(b)(ii)(C/25/27-A/29 of NDPS Act for alleged possession
and transportation of contraband weighing about 113 Kg. 540 grams.
4. The prosecution story is that, one Pick Up van escorted by two other vehicles, i.e. one motor cycle
and one TATA Indica car were coming and seeing the raiding police party, the pillion rider of the
motor cycle, two persons from TATA Indica car and one from the Pick Up vehicle started running by
jumping from the moving vehicle, but they were caught-hold by the police party after good chase.
The contraband ganja was kept in the Pick Up vehicle.
5. It is submitted by Mr. Das, learned Sr. counsel on behalf of the Petitioner that he was an occupant
of the TATA Indica car and he had no connection with the Pick Up van and no contraband was
found from the TATA Indica car.
6. After hearing learned State counsel and considering the circumstances of the case, prima facie
involvement of the Petitioner in the alleged offences cannot be ruled out. Accordingly, I am not
