On an oral prayer made by Sri Awadh Narain Rai, learned counsel for the petitioner, he is permitted
to correct the prayer clause of the petition forthwith.
Heard counsel for the petitioner and Sri Rajesh Kumar Dwivedi, learned counsel appearing for the
State-respondents.
The present petition has been filed seeking a direction to the respondent No. 2, the Tehsildar, Tehsil
Sadar Gorakhpur, District Gorakhpur, to decide Case No. 4986/T202005310317663/07.01.2021
[Sanjay Kumar Singh Vs. Mahatam Singh], under Section 34/35 of the U.P. Revenue Code, 2006,

expeditiously and within a stipulated time period.
It is pointed out that against the order dated 07.01.2021, passed in proceedings under Sections
34/35 of the U.P. Revenue Code, 2006, the petitioner has preferred a restoration application dated
08.01.204, which is pending.
Counsel for the petitioner has referred to the 'order sheet' of the case, a copy whereof has been
appended to the writ petition to point out that although several dates have been fixed but for some
reason or the other, matter has been adjourned. The delay in disposal of the case is stated to be
causing hardship to the petitioner.
Accordingly, a prayer is made for a direction to the respondent No. 2, the Tehsildar, Tehsil Sadar
Gorakhpur, District Gorakhpur, to decide Case No. 4986/T202005310317663/07.01.2021 [Sanjay
Kumar Singh Vs. Mahatam Singh], to conclude the proceedings at an early date.
Having regard to the aforesaid, the present petition is disposed of with an observation that the
respondent No. 2, the Tehsildar, Tehsil Sadar Gorakhpur, District Gorakhpur, to decide Case No.
4986/T202005310317663/07.01.2021 [Sanjay Kumar Singh Vs. Mahatam Singh], would make an
endeavor to conclude the proceedings of the case, which is stated to be pending before it, preferably
within a period of three months, without granting any unnecessary adjournments to either of the
parties, provided there is no other legal impediment.
