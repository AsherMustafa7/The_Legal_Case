1. On 19.03.2025, following order was passed:-
"1. Heard learned counsel for the applicant and Shri Arun Kumar Singh, Advocate
holding brief of Shri Rishabh Tripathi, learned counsel for the respondent.
2. The present application has been filed with the prayer to summon and prosecute
the respondent for willfully and deliberately disobeying the order of Writ Court dated
12.07.2023 passed in Writ A No.4910 of 2020; Mohale Ram Vs. State of U.P. and Ors.

3. As per the office report dated 02.11.2023 based on the report of CJM, Sitapur
dated 01.11.2023, notice has already been served on respondent but till today, no
affidavit of compliance has been filed.
4. Shri Arun Kumar Singh, Advocate prays for one week's time to file affidavit of
compliance.
5. The prayer made is allowed.
6. List this case on 28.03.2025.
7. On the next date, affidavit of compliance shall be filed, failing which, respondent
shall appear in person before this Court for framing of charge."
2. Affidavit of compliance filed by the respondent is taken on record.
3. Sri Arun Kumar Singh, Advocate holding brief of Sri Rishabh Tripathi, learned counsel for the
respondent submits that representation of the applicant has already been decided by the respondent
vide order dated 08.12.2023.
4. Considering the contents of the order passed by the writ Court as well as contents of affidavit of
compliance, this Court is of the view that no contempt is made out. Accordingly, the contempt
application is dismissed.
5. However, it is open to the applicant to take appropriate remedy, if he is feeling aggrieved with the
order of the respondent.
