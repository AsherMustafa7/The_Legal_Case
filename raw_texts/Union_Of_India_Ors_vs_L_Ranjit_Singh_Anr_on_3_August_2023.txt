A. Bimol Singh, J.
Mr. L. Anand, learned counsel and the Secretary of the High Court Bar Association of Manipur
submitted before this court that due to volatile law and order situation that suddenly arose today in
the State of Manipur, many of the advocates could not attend this court today. The learned counsel,
accordingly, made a prayer on behalf of all the members of the Bar Association for adjourning the
cases listed today on another date and for passing appropriate order for extension of the interim
order till the next date.
In view of the submission made above, let these cases be listed again on 04-08-2023.
Interim order, if any, shall stand extended till the next date.
