1. Heard learned counsel for parties.
2. By means of this petition filed under Article 226 of the Constitution, petitioner has prayed for
following substantial reliefs:
"(I) Issue a writ order or direction in the nature of mandamus commanding &
directing the Respondent No. 4 to consider the claim of the petitioner and pass an
order to reinstate in on duty as before.

(II) Issue a writ order or direction in the nature of mandamus commanding &
directing the Respondent No. 4 to consider and decide the representation moved by
the petitioner on 29.10.2024 within the stipulated time period."
3. It transpires from the records that in the above regard petitioner has already represented the
matter before the respondent No. 4 on 29.10.2024 but nothing further happened in the matter.
4. Learned Standing Counsel has obtained instruction in the matter and the same is taken on record.
As per instructions, since the criminal case is pending against the petitioner and such other persons,
the Director General of Youth Welfare and Prantiya Rakshak Dal, U.P. Lucknow has requested the
under Secretary Youth Welfare Department, Government of U.P. Lucknow on 13.01.2025 so that
appropriate orders can be passed.
5. In the given facts and circumstances, this petition stands disposed of with the direction to the
petitioner to approach the Youth Welfare Department, Government of U.P along with certified copy
of this order and in the event petitioner prefers a representation, the state Government shall take
appropriate decision in the matter and issue proper guidelines to the Director General Youth
Welfare and Prantiya Rakshak Dal pursuant to the letter dated 13.1.2025 written by it as to what
should be the steps taken with regard to such volunteers of the Prantiya Rakshak Dal who have been
implicated in a criminal case.
6. Appropriate decision shall be taken by the State Government within a period of two months from
the production of certified copy of this order.
