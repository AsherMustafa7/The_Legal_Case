senior counsel assisted by Mr. S. Gunabanta, learned counsel appearing for respondent No.
1/election petitioner and Mr. Ng. Jotindra Luwang, learned counsel appearing for respondent No. 5.
The present writ petition had been filed seeking leave to allow the applicant to file replication to the
written statement filed by respondent No. 1 in the connected election petition. The learned senior
counsel appearing for respondent No. 1, who is the contesting respondent, submitted that the
applicant has no objection in allowing the present application since the said replication had been
filed within a permissible time.
In view of the submission made above, the present application is allowed.
Registry is directed to put on record the replication filed by the applicant to the written statement of
respondent No. 1 in the connected election petition No. 21 of 2022.
Misc. Case stands disposed of.
