[2] On perusal of memo of appeal and hearing the parties, the appeal is admitted on the following
substantial questions of law.
(a) Whether the order dated 12.01.2016(Annexure-A/6) passed in Execution Case
No.04 of 2014 is a decree within the meaning of Order 21, Rule 103 of the Code of
Civil Procedure, 1908.?
(b) Whether the order dated 12.01.2016 (Annexure-A/6) passed in Execution Case
No.04 of 2014 is appealable or not?

(c) Whether the provisions of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 can supersede/take
over the provisions of the Manipur Land Revenue and Land Reforms Act, 1960?
(d) Whether in the light of the provisions of Sections 51-B, 159 and 160 of the
Manipur Land Revenue and Land Reforms Act, 1960, can the Hon'ble Court of
District Judge, Imphal West forward the decree dated 28.11.2014 along with its
judgment and order and all the necessary papers to the Deputy Commissioner,
Imphal west or to the Director of Settlement and Land Records, Manipur for effective

24.11.2014 passed in Original Suit No.14 of 2010/220 of 2013?
(g) Whether the Executing Court can forward letter of request for partition under Section 54 of the
Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 to the Collector when there is no specific direction made in the
Judgement and decree directing the Collector to effect partition?
(h) Whether the original Suit No.14 of 2010/220 of 2013 was a partition suit or a suit for declaration
and eviction.
