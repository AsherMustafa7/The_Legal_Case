JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 20-02-2023 Heard Mr. Ajoy Pebam, learned counsel
appearing for the petitioner in El. Petn. No. 2 of 2022, Mr. N. Kumarjit, learned senior counsel
assisted by Mr. N. Zequeson, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner in El. Recr. Petn. No. 3 of
2022 and Mr. I. Bikramjit, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner in El. Recr. Petn. No. 4 of
2022.
By an order dated 05-12-2022, this Court had already framed three issues, however, the petitioner
subsequently filed an application being MC(El. Petn.) No. 20 of 2023 with a prayer for framing
additional issues. After hearing the submission of the learned counsel appearing for the parties, the
said application was allowed by an order passed today in the said misc. application. In view of the
order passed today in the said application, the issues are re-framed as hereunder:-
1. Whether the Respondent No. 1 failed to disclose his Agricultural Land (Phourel
Class) being Old Pata No. 85 AP corresponding to New Patta No. 1005 covered by
C.S. Dag No. 5667 measuring an area of 1.09 acres of Village No. 52-Moirang which is
recorded in the name of the Respondent No. 1 as Co-Pattadar along with one person
namely Phairembam Tomba Singh in his Form 26 Affidavit date 04/02/2022?
2. Whether the Respondent No. 1 failed to disclose his Agricultural Land (Phourel
Class) being New Patta No. 442/ 1604 covered by C.S. Dag No. 5730/ 5776 measuring
an area of 0.62 acres of Village No. 52-Moirang in his Form 26 Affidavit dated
04/02/2022?

learned senior counsel assisted by Mr. N. Zequeson, learned counsel appearing for the respondent
No. 2.
The present application had been filed with the prayer for framing additional issues as proposed in
para 5 of the present application over and above the issues framed earlier by this court.
Mr. I. Bikramjit, learned counsel appearing for the respondent No. 1 as well as Mr. N. Kumarjit,
learned counsel appearing for the respondens No. 2 submitted that they have no objection is
allowing the present application. On perusal of the record of the present application, this Court is of
the considered view that instead of adding the issues as proposed by the present applicant in para 5
of this application, it will be just and proper to delete the Issue No. 1 framed by this court on
05-12-2022 and its place, the following issues be added. Accordingly, it is hereby ordered that in
place of the Issue No. 1 framed by this court on 05-12-2022, the following issues will be added:-
