ORDER
16.09.2021 Order No. This matter is taken up through hybrid mode.
2. Heard Mr. D.N. Rath, learned counsel for the petitioner.
3. Mr. D.N. Rath, learned counsel for the petitioner contended that in view of latest judgment of the
apex Court in State of Madhya Pradesh v. Amit Shrivas, AIR 2020 SC4541 : (2020) 10 SCC 496 and
also the order dated 06.09.2021 passed in Special Leave to Appeal (C) No.19252 of 2018 (Seema
Kausar v. State of Maharashtra), the case of the petitioner should be considered for compassionate
appointment.
4. Mr. A.K. Sharma, learned Additional Government Advocate contended that in view of judgment
of the apex Court in N.C. Santosh v. State of Karnataka, (2020) 7 SCC 617, the Division Bench of this
Court has entertained W.A. No.367 of 2021, which has been preferred by the State against the order
passed by the learned Single Judge directing to consider the case of the petitioner keeping in view
the judgment passed by the apex Court in Canara Bank v. M. Mahesh Kumar, (2015) 7 SCC 412.
