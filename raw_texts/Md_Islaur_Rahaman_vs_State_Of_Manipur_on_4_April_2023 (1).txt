BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A. GUNESHWAR SHARMA Order 04.04.2023 Present Mr. I.
Bikramjit, learned counsel for the appellant and Mr. H. Samarjit, learned PP for the State
respondents.
Vide order dated 08.12.2022 passed by the Addl. Sessions Judge (FTC), Manipur West in Sessions
Trial Case No. 2 of 2017, the appellant (herein accused No. 2) was sentenced to undergo rigorous
imprisonment for 2 (two) years with a fine of Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) under Section
451/34 IPC and vide another order dated 08.12.2022 in Cril.Misc.Case No. 2 of 2022, the learned
Trial Court has suspended the sentence and released on bail for enabling to file the present Appeal.
The appellant prays for passing an appropriate order under Section 389(1) and the sentence
imposed may be suspended during the pendency of the present appeal.
Prayer is allowed.
The order of sentence dated 08.12.2022 in Sessions Trail Case No. 2 of 2017 is suspended during the
pendency of the appeal on the same conditions.
Appeal is admitted.
Fix on 03.05.2023 along with Crl.A. No. 19 of 2022. Registry is directed to prepare paper book. No
separate paper book is required in the present case and if required, the parties may point out the
documents in the supplementary paper book, if any.
