BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.GUNESHWAR SHARMA 22.2.2023 Heard Mr.H.Kenajit,
learned counsel for the appellant. The present appeal has been filed under sub Section (2) of Section
374 of the Cr.P.C for setting aside the impugned judgment dated 9.2.2023 and sentence dated
13.02.2023 passed by the learned Sessions Judge, Thoubal in Sessions Trial Case No.25 of 2014/12
of 2022.
By the impugned order, the appellant and another co- accused were convicted under Sections
366/511/34 IPC and were sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment (RI) for two years with fine
of Rs.5000/- and in default another simple imprisonment of six months.
The appeal is admitted.
Issue notice to the State.
Mr.Y.Ashang, learned PP accepts notice on behalf of the State and no formal notice is required.
Call for the LCR.
Along with the appeal, the appellant has filed an application under Section 389 Cr.P.C for
suspending the operation of the conviction and sentence orders dated 9.2.2023 and 13.2.2023
passed by the learned Sessions Judge, Thoubal in Trial Case No.24 of 2014/12 of 2022.
Mr.Kenajit, learned counsel for the appellant points out that as per Sub-Section (3) of Section 389 of
the Cr.P.C it is mandatory for the Court to release the convict person on bail if the sentence is less
than three years and the convict intends to file an appeal. He draws attention of this Court to the
Indian Kanoon - http://indiankanoon.org/doc/44143882/ 1

sentence order dated 13.02.2023 where both the convicts were sentenced to undergo RI for two
years and the present case is fully covered by Sub Section (3) of Section 389 of the Cr.P.C.
Heard Mr.Y.Ashang, learned PP on this point. This Court is of the considered opinion that if the
sentence is not exceeded than three years and the convict intends to file appeal and therefore, the
Trial Court has to exercise jurisdiction under Sub Section (3) of Section 389 of the Cr.P.C.
Accordingly, the order of sentence dated 13.02.2023 is suspended for the appellant herein during
the pendency of the present appeal. The appellant, Sanasam Binoy @ Inee Singh, aged about 46
years, s/o S.Bode Singh of Sabalgongba, Mayai Leikai, PO PS & District, Thoubal, Manipur be
released on bail on the previous condition and bond to the satisfaction of the learned Sessions
Judge, Thoubal. Accordingly, MC (Crl A) No.4 of 2023 is allowed and disposed of.
Send a copy of this order to the learned Sessions Judge, Thoubal for information and for doing the
needful.
Copy of this order be given to learned counsel for the parties.
