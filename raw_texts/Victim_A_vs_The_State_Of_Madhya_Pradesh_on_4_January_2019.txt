The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
MCRC-44733-2018
(VICTIM (A) Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)
Jabalpur, Dated : 04-01-2019
Shri A.K. Kurmi, learned counsel for the applicant.
Shri Punit Shroti, learned P.L. for the respondent No.1-State.
Heard on the question of admission.
On due consideration of the statement of prosecutrix (PW-2) and the material available on record,
we are of the view that, it is a fit case in which permission for grant of leave to appeal can be allowed
in respect of respondents No.2 and 3, meaning thereby, the matter has to be admitted for final
hearing.
Accordingly, application filed by the applicant under Section 378(3) of Cr.P.C. is allowed and
permission for grant of leave to appeal is granted.
As a consequence of this order, this MCRC be registered as Criminal Appeal and proceeded as per
rules.
As Mr. Shroti, Panel Lawyer, is appearing on behalf of the respondent No.1- State, therefore, no
notice is required to be issued.
Issue notice to the respondents No. 2 and 3 only, on payment of P.F. within a period of one week,
returnable within 6 weeks.
O n payment of requisite process fee, office is directed to issue bailable warrant of
Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand) against the respondents No.2 and 3. They are also directed to
furnish a bail bond in the sum of Rs.10,000/- with one surety each in the like amount to the
satisfaction of the CJM/Trial court for their appearance before the Registry/Office of this court on
11.04.2019 and on all other subsequent dates as may be fixed by the office in this behalf.
With the aforesaid, M.Cr.C.No.44733/2018 is allowed and disposed of.
(P.K. JAISWAL) (RAJENDRA KUMAR SRIVASTAVA)
JUDGE JUDGE
