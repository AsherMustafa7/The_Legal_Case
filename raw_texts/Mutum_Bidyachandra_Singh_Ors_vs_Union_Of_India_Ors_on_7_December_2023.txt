B E F O R E HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 07-12-2023 Heard Mr. W.
Shantikumar, learned counsel appearing for the applicants, Mr. S. Samarjeet, learned senior panel
counsel appearing for the respondent No. 1 and Ms. Harichhaya, learned counsel appearing for the
respondents No. 2 to 6.
The present application had been filed with a prayer for setting aside the order dated 12-11-2021
passed by this court in WP(C) No. 1029 of 2015 thereby dismissing the said writ petition for
non-prosecution.
Mr. S. Samarjeet, learned senior panel counsel and Ms. Harichyaya, learned counsel appearing for
the respondents fairly submitted that they have no objection in allowing the present restoration
application.
After hearing the submissions advanced by the learned counsel appearing for the parties and on
perusal of the record, this court is satisfied that sufficient ground has been made out for recalling
the order dated 12-11-2021 passed by this court dismissing WP(C) No. 1029 of 2015 for
non-prosecution.
Accordingly, the present application is hereby allowed by quashing and setting aside the aforesaid
order passed by this court.
Registry is directed to restore on record the file of WP(C) No. 1029 of 2015 for further hearing.
