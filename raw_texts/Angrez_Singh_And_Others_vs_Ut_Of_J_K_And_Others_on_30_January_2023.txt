ORDER
30.01.2023 Brief facts of the case is that the petitioners are joint owners in possession of the land
falling under khasra No.600/585/202 measuring 30 kanals which is recorded as Shamlat deh in the
revenue record situated at Village Hunder, Tehsil Arnas, District Reasi. It is averred in the petition
that the land is in the possession of the petitioner and is used by them for cultivation as well as for
making fodder for the livestock.
It is further averred in the petition that the respondents 3 and 4 are construction a road from Narloo
to Dugender via Bareli under NABARD scheme.
It is the contention of the petitioner that the land of the petitioners have not been proposed for
acquisition nor their land has come in the alignment of the road but still the respondents are taking
the possession of the land of the petitioner without following the due process of law.
Notice.

This writ petition is disposed of by issuing a direction to the respondents that they shall not take
possession of the land of the petitioners falling under Khasra No.600/585/202 measuring 30 kanals
situated at Village Hunder, Tehsil Arnas, District Reasi without following the due process of law.
