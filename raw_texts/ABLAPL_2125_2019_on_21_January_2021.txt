ABLAPL No. 2125 of 2019
5. 21.01.2021 This matter is taken up through video conferencing.
Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and
Mr.Kanungo, learned Additional Government Advocate.
The petitioners are allegedly involved in C.T.Case No.
2556 of 2018 arising out of Nayapalli P.S. Case No. 237 of 2018
pending in the court of learned S.D.J.M., Bhubaneswar for commission
of offence under Sections 294/295-A/323/386/406/452/
427/447/327/354/395/420/468/469/471/506/34/120-B of the
I.P.C.
Learned counsel for the petitioners submitted that one
Gita Sinha complainant has filed I.C.C. case no. 1422 of 2018 against
the petitioners before the learned S.D.J.M., Bhubaneswar which was
subsequently registered as Nayapalli P.S. Case No. 237 of 2018.
Similarly a counter case has been filed by the present petitioners i.e.
I.C.C. Case No. 1386 of 2018 against the husband of the complainant
and subsequently the same was registered as Nayapalli P.S. Case No.
159 of 2018 for commission of offence under Sections
307/387/342/323/451/354/392/406/294/506/34 of the I.P.C. Case
and counter case has been filed.
Considering the aforesaid submissions and the interim
protection was granted to the petitioners on 13.3.2019 as well as to
expedite the trial, this Court directs that in case the petitioners
surrender before the learned S.D.J.M., Bhubaneswar in connection
with C.T.Case No. 2556 of 2018 and moves for bail in such event they
shall be released on bail on such terms and conditions as deemed fit
and proper with an undertaking that they shall appear before the
Court as and when necessary and cooperate for early conclusion of the
trial.
The ABLAPL is accordingly disposed of.
