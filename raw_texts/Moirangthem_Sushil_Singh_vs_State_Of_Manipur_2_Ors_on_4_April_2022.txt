BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MV MURALIDARAN 04.04.2022 Heard Mr. H.S. Paonam,
learned senior counsel for the petitioner and Mr. H. Samarjit, learned GA who takes notice for all
the respondents. [2] The writ petition has been filed challenging the suspension order dated
2.09.2021.
[3] Mr. H.S. Paonam, learned senior counsel for the petitioner argued the case on merit and for
interim order, he represented that the petitioner was suspended on 2.09.2021 as per the impugned
order, but till date, the subsistence allowance has not been paid by the respondent authority.
Therefore, he prayed this Court that suitable direction may be issued to the Respondents to pay the
subsistence allowance to the petitioner from 02.09.2021.
Page - 2 [4] Mr. H. Samarjit, learned GA who takes notice for the Respondents submitted that he
wants to get instruction from the Respondents and report this Court.
