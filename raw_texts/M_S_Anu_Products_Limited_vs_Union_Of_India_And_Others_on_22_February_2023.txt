ORDER
22.02.2023 Notice.
Mr. Rohan Nanda, learned counsel accepts notice on behalf of the respondents. He seeks and is
granted four weeks' time to file response.
Mr. Vikrant Kackria, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner submits that in an identical
petition bearing WP(C) No.2793/2022 title 'M/s UPL Limited vs. Union of India and others, this
Court vide order dated 26.12.2022 has granted interim relief. He seeks the same interim relief as has
been granted in above identical petition.
Though, Mr. Rohan Nanda, learned counsel appearing for the respondents has resisted for grant of
interim relief, however in order to maintain parity, we propose to grant the same interim relief.
Therefore, subject to objections from the other side and till next date of hearing, the operation of
impugned order dated 30.12.2022, appended as Annexure-I with the petition, shall stay.
List on 28.04.2023 along with WP(C) No.2793/2022.
