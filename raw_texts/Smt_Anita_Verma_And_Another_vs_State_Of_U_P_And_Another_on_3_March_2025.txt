1. Heard Sri Manoj Kumar Tiwari, learned counsel for the applicants, Sri Sudhir Kumar, learned
AGA for the State.
2. This is an application U/s 528 of BNSS preferred for quashing of the impugned order dated
25.09.2024, 15.10.2024, 04.11.2024, 04.12.2024 and 17.10.2025 passed in warrant or summons in
Criminal Case No. 41/2023 passed by the court of Special Judge, SC/ST Prevention of Atrocities Act,
Ballia by which the learned trial court issued non-bailable warrant without issuing proper summons
against the applicants for the offence punishable under Sections 323, 504 IPC and Section 3(1)Da,
Dha and 3(1)v Ka SC/ST Act.
3. Learned AGA has raised a preliminary objection regarding maintainability of the application on
the ground that a criminal appeal no.9700 of 2024 (Smt. Anita verma and another vs. State of U.P.)
has been preferred by the applicant in which on 01.10.2024, learned AGA was directed to seek
instruction. According which the present application would not be maintainable.
4. On a pointed query be raised, as to what was the subject matter of challenging the said appeal to
which learned counsel for the applicants submits that the entire proceedings as well as warrants
against the other applicants herein has been subject matter of challenge.

5. Having heard learned counsel for the parties, I am of the opinion that the relief which has been
sought cannot be granted to the applicants in the present proceedings in application under Section
482 of Cr.PC No. 8635 of 2023 (Abhishek Awasthi alias Bholu Awasthi vs. State of U.P. & another)
decided on 26.11.2024 wherein the entire proceedings has not been challenged and further appeal is
already pending in which appropriate application can be preferred.
6. With the aforesaid observation, the application is consigned to records.
Order Date :- 3.3.2025/C. MANI (Vikas Budhwar,J.)
