B E F O R E HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 27-02-2023 Mr. N. Zequeson,
learned counsel appeared in person and submitted that he had already filed an affidavit showing
proof of service of notice upon the respondent No. 1 by speed post. The Learned counsel further
submitted that if this Court is of the view that the service of notice is not adequate, he will take fresh
steps for service of notice upon respondent no. 1 by way of dasti service and accordingly, learned
counsel prayed for granting one week's time for taking fresh steps.
Prayer is allowed.
Applicant is directed to take fresh steps for service of notice upon the respondent No. 1 by way of
dasti service and file an affidavit showing proof of such service.
As prayed for, list this case again on 20-03-2023.
