ORDER
01. In the instant petition, the petitioners have invoked the jurisdiction of this Court under Section
482 Cr.P.C. seeking quashment of FIR No.0118 of 2023 dated 07.08.2023 lodged by respondent
No.1 for commission of offences under Sections 120-B, 193, 196, 195, 408, 209, 211, 323, 327, 330,
342, 347, 348, 352, 357, 384, 385, 386, 387, 392, 403, 420, 465, 467, 468, 471, 474, 500, 504, 506
and 34 IPC against the petitioners.
02. Learned counsel for the petitioners states that respondent No.2 against the dismissal of his
application under Section 156(3) Cr.P.C. passed by Learned Special Excise Mobile Magistrate,
Jammu by order dated 02.03.2023, filed a criminal revision petition before Learned Principal
Sessions Judge, Jammu and vide impugned judgment and order dated 15.07.2023, the learned
Principal Sessions Judge, Jammu has allowed the revision petition of respondent No.2 and directed
the trial Court to send the original complaint to respondent No.1 for registration of FIR on the
aforesaid complaint.
03. It is stated that aggrieved of the judgment/order dated 15.07.2023 passed by Learned Sessions
Judge, Jammu, the petitioners challenged the same by way of petition under section 482 Cr.P.C.,
setting aside the aforesaid judgment passed in criminal revision by Learned Principal Sessions
Judge, Jammu and the petition was numbered as CRM(M) No.646/23 and vide order dated
04.08.2023, this Court had stayed the operation of judgment dated 15.07.2023 passed by the
