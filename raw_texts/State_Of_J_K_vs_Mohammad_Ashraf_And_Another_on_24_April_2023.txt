ORDER
1. The present application has been filed by the applicant seeking condonation of delay in filing the
appeal against the judgment of acquittal recorded by the court of learned Sessions Judge,
Udhampur whereby the respondents have been acquitted of the charges for commission of offences
under Section 8/15 NDPS Act in FIR No. 15/2011 registered with Police Station, Kud.
2. Despite service, respondents have chosen not to file response.
3. For the reasons stated in the application, the same is allowed. Delay in filing the appeal is
condoned.
4. Application stands disposed of.
1. This is an application seeking leave of this Court to file appeal against the judgment of acquittal
dated 09.07.2014 recorded by the court of learned Sessions Judge, Udhampur whereby the
respondents have been acquitted c/w of the charges for commission of offences under Section 8/15
NDPS Act in FIR No. 15/2011 registered with Police Station, Kud.
2. Having regard to the issues raised in the present application, this Court is of the considered view
that the evidence is required to be appreciated and that can be done only in the event leave is
granted to the applicant to file the appeal. Accordingly applicant is granted leave to file appeal
against the judgment of acquittal recorded by the court of learned Sessions Judge, Udhampur
whereby the respondents have been acquitted of the charges for commission of offences under
