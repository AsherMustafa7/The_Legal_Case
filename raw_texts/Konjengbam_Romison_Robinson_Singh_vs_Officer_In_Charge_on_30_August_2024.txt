ORDER
30.08.2024 Heard Mr. L. Gunindro, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and Mr. Y.
Ashang, learned PP appearing for the respondents.
The present petition has been filed by the petitioner under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal
Procedure, 1973 with the following prayers:
i. To issue Rule nisi ii. To set aside/quash the FIR registered against the petitioner
under FIR No. 271 (10)2022IPS U/S 448/506/34 IPC. iii. To issue/pass any other
order(s)/ direction(s) which the Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts
and circumstances of the case.
During the course of hearing, the learned counsel for the petitioner submits a letter
dated 09.08.2024 addressed to the Additional Government Advocate, Manipur by
Officer-in- Charge, Imphal Police Station mentioning that the investigation in c/w
W.P. (C) No. 23 of 2023 was closed on 30.04.2024 by returning in Final Report (FR)
vide FR No. 11/IPS/2024 dated 30.04.2024 on the ground of "insufficiency of
evidence" and the same is extracted hereunder:
"GOVERNMENT OF MANIPUR OFFICE OF THE OFFICER-IN-CHARGE IMPHAL
POLICE STATION, IMPHAL WEST DISTRICT, MANIPUR To The Additional
Government Advocate Manipur.
