BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.GUNESHWAR SHARMA 27.12.2023 Heard Mr.H.S.Paonam,
learned senior counsel assisted by Mr.Sushil Daga, learned counsel for the petitioner.
The petitioner has challenged the termination order dated 20.12.2023 issued by the Chief Engineer,
PHED, Manipur. Mr.A.Golly, learned counsel appears on behalf of the respondent No.2, Chief
Executive Officer, PHED, along with Caveat Petition No.119 of 2023.
Caveat stands discharged.
Also present Mr.S.Nepolean, learned Government Advocate assisted by Ms.R.K.Emily, learned Dy.
Government Advocate appears on behalf of respondent Nos.1 and 3.
Mr.Paonam, learned senior counsel prays for stay of the termination order dated 20.12.2023 as the
same was in violation of the conditions of the contract as well as the provisions of the Contract Act.
Mr.A.Golly, learned counsel for respondent No.2/Caveator submits that this matter may be listed on
a next date as the caveat is discharged only today.
Mr.S.Nepolean, learned Government Advocate submits that the contract was terminated after giving
due notice on the ground of delay in execution of the work and prays that no interim order may be
passed and the project is time bound and no coercive action will be taken against the petitioner till
the next date.
Mr.Paonam, learned senior counsel clarified that the contract is valid till 16.12.2024. Mr.Nepolean,
learned Government Advocate submits that only 26% work progress is done by the petitioner and
