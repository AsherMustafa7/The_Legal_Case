B E F O R E HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AHANTHEM BIMOL SINGH 21-11-2023 Heard Mr. Ajoy
Pebam, learned counsel appearing for the applicant, Mr. N. Ibotombi, learned senior counsel
assisted by Mr. A. Rommel, learned counsel appearing for the respondent No. 1 and Mr. N.
Kumarjit, learned senior counsel assisted by Mr. N. Zequeson, learned counsel appearing for the
respondent No. 2.
As agreed to by both the counsel appearing for the parties, the present application is being disposed
of at the Order stage itself.
At the outset, Mr. Ajoy Pebam, learned counsel appearing for the applicant made a prayer for
allowing the applicant to withdraw the prayer No. (ii) with liberty to file a separate application
seeking such prayer and to allow the petitioner to pursue the present application in respect of the
first prayer only.
This prayer is not objected to by both the learned senior counsel appearing for the respondents.
Accordingly, the said prayer is allowed with liberty as sought for.
The present application had been filed praying for correcting the typographical mistake occurred in
the order dated 07-11-2023 passed by this court in MC(El. Petn.) No. 70 of 2023. The necessary
correction is as under:-
" In the first line of the said order, the name of the counsel for the applicant, which
has been recorded as "Mr. Prithiviraj"
should be substituted by "Mr. Riyanandan Nongmaithem"
