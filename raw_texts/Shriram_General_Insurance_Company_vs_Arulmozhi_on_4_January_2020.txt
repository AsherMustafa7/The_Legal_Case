https://www.mhc.tn.gov.in/judis TERMS OF SETTLEMENT The Tribunal has
awarded a sum of Rs.14,13,125/- with interest at 7.5% per annum from the date of
petition till the date of decree and costs as compensation. Aggrieved by the award of
the Tribunal, the appellant / Insurance Company had preferred the present appeal.
2. After due deliberation and consultation, both the parties have agreed to modify the
award to a sum of Rs.15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only) in full quit (inclusive
of interest) as compensation.
3. The appellant / Insurance Company is directed to deposit the above said accepted
award amount of Rs.15,00,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Lakhs only), after deducting the
amount already deposited if any, within a period of Six weeks from the date of receipt
of a copy of this award. On such deposit, the first respondent is entitled to withdraw
