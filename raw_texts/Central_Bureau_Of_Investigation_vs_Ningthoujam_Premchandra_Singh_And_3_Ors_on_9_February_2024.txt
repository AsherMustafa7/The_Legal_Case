BEFORE HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE GOLMEI GAIPHULSHILLU 09.02.2024 Heard, Mr. W.
Darakishwor, learned Sr. PCCG appearing for the applicant and Ms. L. Ashapriya, learned counsel
appearing for the respondents.
The learned Sr. PCCG for the applicant submitted that he filed misc. application for extension of
another 4 (four) months' time from the date of expiry i.e. 01.02.2024 for completion of investigation
by Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) in case no. RC 3(S)/2022-IMP relating to murder of
Ningthoujam Rohit Singh.
Further, the learned Sr. PCCG submits that vide order dated 29.07.2022 passed in Cril. Petition No.
6 of 2022, this Court directed to hand over the investigation of the case to CBI and in the order, it
was directed by this Court as follows:
"The criminal petition is accordingly allowed, directing the CBI to take up the
investigation in F.I.R. No. 34(12) 2021 HRK-PS, registered under Sections 458, 326,
302 & 34 IPC read with Section 25(1-C) of the Arms Act, 1959. The State authorities
shall entrust all the case records pertaining to the said F.I.R. to the CBI forthwith and
extend full cooperation in all respects to the CBI in the course of its investigation. The
CBI shall endeavor to complete the investigation expeditiously and preferably, within
6 (six) months from the date of receipt of a copy of this order."
The learned Sr. PCCG submits that an application has been filed for extension of another 6 (six)
months' time to complete the investigation. The same has been allowed by this Court for extension
of another 6 (six) months' time w.e.f. 01.08.2023 to complete the investigation and file a proper
report before the competent Court, vide order dated 07.08.2023 passed in MC (Cril. Petn.) No. 11 of
2023 as follows:
"[7] The application is allowed and CBI is given another 6 (six) months' time w.e.f.
01.08.2023 to complete the investigation and file a proper report before the

competent Court."
Moreover, after going through the misc. application filed the learned Sr. PCCG and after perusing
the Court's order as reflected above and this Court is of the view that some time has been given for
the investigating authority to serve the purpose of both the parties.
Accordingly, the prayer for extension of 4 (four) months' time w.e.f. 01.02.2024 is allowed and it is
directed to the learned Sr. PCCG for the applicant, to inform the authority that this given time of 4
(four) months will be utilized properly and to finish the investigation and submit the report without
fail.
Furnish a copy of this order to the learned counsel appearing for both the parties.
The present MC (Cril. Petn.) No. 8 of 2024 is disposed of.
