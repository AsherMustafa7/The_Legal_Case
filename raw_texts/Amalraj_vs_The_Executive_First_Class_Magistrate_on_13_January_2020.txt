ORDER
This Criminal Revision Case has been filed to call for the entire records pertaining to the impugned
detention order passed in M.C.No.44/2018/A1 by the first respondent/Executive First Class
Magistrate Cum Revenue Divisional Officer, Dindigul, dated 27.09.2018.
2.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that as per the impugned order, dated
27.09.2018, the petitioner has to keep in jail from 27.09.2018 till 03.07.2019. Now almost six
months have been lapsed and hence due to the lapse of period, the prayer sought for by the
petitioner has become infrcutuous.
3.Recording the submission made by the learned Additional Public Prosecutor, this Criminal
Revision Case is dismissed as infrcutuous.
