1. List of fresh cases has been revised. No one appeared on behalf of the applicants. However,
learned AGA for the State is present.
2. It is informed by learned AGA that the Bar Association of Allahabad High Court has passed a
resolution that all the members of Bar will abstain from judicial work.
3. Present application has been filed for quashing the entire proceeding as well as charge sheet dated
20.02.2010 in Case No.309 of 2010 arising out of Case Crime No.01 of 2010, under Sections 498-A,
323, 506 IPC and D.P. Act, Police Station Mahila Thana, District Agra.
4. From perusal of record, it appears that dispute between the parties is matrimonial in nature and
both the parties have settled their dispute amicably and entered into compromise. The aforesaid
compromise dated 05.12.2024 is reduced in written compromise and also annexed at page 32 of the

affidavit. It has been requested that the impugned proceedings may be quashed on the basis of
aforesaid compromise.
5. Though this fact has not been disputed by learned AGA but he has raised a preliminary objection
that the aforesaid proceeding cannot be quashed on the basis of compromise. The aforesaid
compromise should be verified by the court below before quashing the impugned proceeding.
6. In view of the above, the applicants are directed to file original deed of the compromise before the
court below within a period of 15 days from today. On filing of such compromise, the court below
will verify the same within a period of two months after summoning the parties.
7. For a period of two months or till verification of compromise, whichever is earlier, no coercive
action shall be taken against the applicants in the aforesaid case.
8. However, the parties are at liberty to file a fresh application under Section 528 BNSS to quash the
impugned proceeding on the basis of verified copy of compromise, if need arises.
9. With the aforesaid direction, the present application is disposed of.
