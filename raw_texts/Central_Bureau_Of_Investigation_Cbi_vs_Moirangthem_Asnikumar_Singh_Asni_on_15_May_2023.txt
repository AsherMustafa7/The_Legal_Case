BEFORE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A. GUNESHWAR SHARMA 15.05.2023 Heard Mr. W.
Darakeshwar, learned Sr. PCCG for the applicant. Vide order dated 17.10.2022 passed by this Court
in CRIL.PETN. No. 31 of 2021, being unsatisfied with the investigation carried out by the State
authorities, the CBI was directed to take over investigation in relation to the F.I.R. No. 188 (06)
2009 Imphal Police Station under Sections 364, 364(A), 307, 457, 302 & 120-B IPC, read with
Section 25(1-B) of the Arms Act, 1959, and Sections 16 & 20 of Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act,
1967 and directed to complete the investigation preferably within a period of 6(six) months.
It is submitted that the period of 6(six) months has already expired on 19.04.2023 and the present
application was filed on 03.05.2023 with a prayer for seeking extension of another 6(six) months'
time with effect from 19.04.2023.
Issue notice to the respondent.
Mr. W. Darakeshwar, learned Sr. PCCG, prays for serving the notice by dasti to the sole respondent.
Fix on 22.05.2023.
