ORDER
23.02.2023 [1] Heard Mr. M. Gunedhor, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and Mr.
Shyam Sharma, learned GA for the State respondents. [2] It is the case of the petitioner that
originally, the petitioner approached the Court of Session Judge, Imphal West and filed Cril.
Misc.(AB) case No. 271 of 2022 and considering the case of the petitioner as well as the prosecution,
the same was closed by directing the respondents to comply the provisions laid under section 41 and
41 A of the CrPC thereby giving prior notice of 3 days to the petitioner, in case the arrest of the
petitioner is required in due course of the investigation. But the petitioner approached this Court by
way of writ petition under Article 226 of the constitution of India by stating there is an
apprehension that originally GD Entry was made and thereafter FIR was made.
[3] Anyhow, this Court cannot considers the writ petition at this stage that too without receiving any
instruction from the respondents and Mr. Shyam Sharma, learned GA is directed to get appropriate
instruction and report this Court on 27.02.2023.
