Heard learned Counsel for the parties.
The present application has been filed for the following main relief:-
"(i) Issue a writ, order or direction in the nature of Mandamus thereby directing the
concerned opposite party no.4 (Additional Collector (Finance and Revenue),
Sultanpur) to investigate the validity of the old Gata number 714 and 723 and
Khatauni Account 92 and 155 of the original year in compliance of the order dated
11.03.2025 passed in Writ-C No.2304/2025 as well as direct the present

Consolidation Officer Lambhua District Sultanpur, who submitted the report in the

(ii) Issue a writ, order or direction in the nature of Mandamus thereby directing the
concerned opposite party no.2/Board of Revenue, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow to
constitute an enquiry committee for conducting departmental inquiry against the
opposite party no.4 (Additional Collector (Finance and Revenue), Sultanpur) for

interest of the State Government and village assembly, in the interest of justice."
At the very outset, the learned Counsel for the petitioner states that he does not want to press the
present petition and the same may be dismissed as withdrawn.
To the aforesaid prayer, learned Counsel for the side opposite has no objection.
Accordingly, the present petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
