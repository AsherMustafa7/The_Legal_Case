4. The present application has been filed to quash the chargesheet no.202 of 2021, dated 07.06.2021
as well as cognizance order dated 20.09.2021 as well as proceeding of Criminal Case No.9488 of
2021, arising out of Case Crime No.245 of 2021, under Sections-498-A, 323, 504, 506 IPC & Section

5. From perusal of record, it appears that prior to the present case, the applicants have also
approached this court through Application u/s 482 No.25513 of 2022 against the impugned
proceeding which was disposed of vide order dated 05.09.2022 directing the parties to appear
before the Mediation Centre of concerned district with further liberty to approach this court again
on the basis of report of the Mediation Centre. It also transpires from the record that parties have
settled their dispute on the basis of compromise, application u/s 13B of the Hindu Marriage Act on
27.02.2024 which is still pending.
6. In the above application filed u/s 13B of Hindu Marriage Act in paragraph no.8, it is clearly
mentioned that the opposite party no.2 will withdraw all cases including the impugned proceeding
in pursuance of the compromise.
7. Considering these facts, present application is disposed of with a direction to the applicants to file
original deed of compromise before the court below, within a period of 15 days. In case, such
compromise is filed before the court below, the court below will verify the same after summoning
the parties within a period of two months from the date of receiving the copy of compromise.
8. It is further provided that for a period of two and a half months or till the verification of
compromise, whichever is earlier, no coercive action shall be taken against the applicants.
9. It is further provided that applicants are at liberty to file fresh application u/s 528 of BNSS, to
quash the impugned proceeding on the basis of verified compromise.
