" That under the facts and circumstances of the case, it is very respectfully prayed
that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to allow this petition and quash the
impugned charge sheet dated 02.11.2019, bearing no. 148 of 2019, Under Section
323, 452, 506, 507 IPC Police Station Patti, District Pratapgarh, contained and
annexed as annexure no. 1 to this petition. further prayed that the entire proceedings
of Criminal Case No. 269 of 2025 State of U.P. Vs. Sanju Sharma alias Dheeraj
Sharma and others, pending before Judicial Magistrate Court no. 24, Pratapgarh its
relates so far the petitioner on the basis of compromise dated 20.3.2025, in the
interest of justice. "
Learned counsel for the parties have jointly submitted that the parties have amicably settled their
dispute out of the Court and they have entered into a compromise by means of compromise deed
dated 20.03.2025. Copy of which has been enclosed as annexure no.4 to the present application.
Learned counsel for the applicant has prayed that a liberty may be given to the applicant to file the
original copy of the compromise along with the application before the trial court within a period of
one week for the purpose of verification of compromise dated 20.03.2025 entered into between the
parties and the court below may be directed to verify the compromise.
As prayed, a week's time is granted to the applicant to file the original copy of the compromise
before the Court below and the court below shall verify the same on 10.04.2025. On the said date all
the signatories to the compromise executed on 20.03.2025 shall appear before the court concerned.
The applicant shall file an affidavit bringing on record the certified copy of the order of verification
passed by the concerned Court by the next date of listing.
List this case in the week commencing 21.04.2025.
Till the next date of listing, no coercive action shall be taken against the applicant.
When the case is listed next, name of Shri Ram Kripal Yadav, Advocate be shown in the list as
counsel for the respondent no. 2.
Office is directed to return the original compromise deed dated 20.03.2025 appended with the
present case to the learned counsel for the applicants while retaining the photocopy of the same.
