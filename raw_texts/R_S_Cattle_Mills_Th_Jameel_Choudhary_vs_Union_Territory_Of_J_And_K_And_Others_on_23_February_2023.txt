ORDER
The petitioner is stated to be registered Firm under GST with State Taxes Circle-Q, Jammu having
registration No. 01AGGPC680IMIZR. The petitioner's Firm was not functioning, as the same was
under installation and due to COVID- 19 pandemic the petitioner could not file GSTR-38 returns
and the respondents for non-filing of the returns passed an order No. ZA011019005252 dated
28.10.2019, whereby the registration of the petitioner under GST with State Taxes Circle-Q, Jammu,
has been cancelled without affording an opportunity of being heard. The petitioner questions the
said cancellation through the medium of the present writ petition.
At this stage, Mr. D.C. Raina, learned Advocate General submits that instead of keeping the petition
pending, the same can be disposed of by giving a liberty to the petitioner to file a detailed
representation, which the respondents shall consider of course, under rules to which learned
counsel for the petitioner is not averse to such proposition.
With the consent of learned counsel for the parties, the instant petition is disposed of by giving a
liberty to the petitioner to approach the authority by filing a detailed representation within one week
from today. If any such representation is filed by the petitioner, the respondents shall reconsider
