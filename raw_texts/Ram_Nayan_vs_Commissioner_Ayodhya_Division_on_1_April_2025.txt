Ram Nayan vs Commissioner, Ayodhya Division, ... on 1 April, 2025
The instant petition has been preferred seeking following main relief:-
"ISSUE an order or direction to Commissioner, Ayodhya Division, Ayodhya bearing
Appeal Case No.2983/2023 U/s 24 (4) U.P. R. Code Styled as Ram Chandar .....Vs....
Ram Nayan and others filed by the opposite party no. 2 against the petitioner and
others within early period prescribed by this Hon'ble Court."
Taking note of Rule 183 (4) of the U.P. Revenue Code Rules, 2016 made under U.P. Revenue Code,
2006 and para 458(2) of U.P. Revenue Court Manual, as per which, revision or appeal should
preferably be decided within six months, however, in the instant case, the revision is pending since
December, 2023 and on account of pendency of the same, the petitioner, as stated, is suffering great
hardship and mental agony, this petition is disposed of with a direction to the opposite party
No.1/Commissioner, Ayodhya Mandal, Ayodhya to consider and decide the Appeal No.2983/2023,
U/s 24 (4) of the U.P. Revenue Code, 2006 (Styled as Ram Chandar .....Vs.... Ram Nayan and
others), expeditiously, after affording full opportunity of hearing to the parties to the litigation and
without granting unnecessary adjournment to either of the parties, preferably within a period of six
months from the next date fixed in the case, if there is no other legal impediment in this regard.
It is made clear that the Court has not examined the case of either party on merits and the Authority
concerned shall be free to decide the matter strictly in accordance with law.
With the aforesaid, the petition is disposed of.
